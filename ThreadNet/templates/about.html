About ThreadNet

ThreadNet is a microblogging platform designed to let users share short messages called tweets, fostering quick and open communication. The platform focuses on providing a simple, user-friendly interface where people can express their thoughts, engage with others' content, and search for tweets. It mirrors the essence of modern social media, allowing users to stay connected and informed.

The platform is built using Django, a powerful Python web framework, ensuring scalability, security, and efficient handling of user data. ThreadNet supports essential social networking functionalities, including user authentication, tweet creation, and real-time search capabilities. Users can share text and images within their tweets, enhancing their ability to communicate visually and interactively.
